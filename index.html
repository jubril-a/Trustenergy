The company, Trust Energy, is owned 100% by a group of multicultural African oil and gas experts and business executives, who have a primary focus to invest their vast experience in Senegal.

With over 80 years of combined industry and business experience, the company aims to invest in developing the human and technical skills of the Senegalese economy and also employing and training the teeming youth population.
