About Trust Energy Group

The company, Trust Energy, is owned 100% by a group of multicultural African oil and gas experts and business executives, who have a primary focus to invest their vast experience in Senegal. With over 80 years of combined industry and business experience, the company aims to invest in developing the human and technical skills of the Senegalese economy and also employing and training the teeming youth population.

With a very strong knowledge of the Sangomar political and geological models, Trust Energy, control by indigenous Oil & Gas professionals with valuable experience and credentials, is prepared essentially to be an excellent Local Content Partner and aims to become a world-class standard oil & gas asset evaluation, field development planning, and petroleum resources management company, and providing Senegalese local capability for the total life cycle exploitation of petroleum resources and components of the chain depending on the point of engagement of Asset, to maximize value to the community, partners, clients, and personnel.
